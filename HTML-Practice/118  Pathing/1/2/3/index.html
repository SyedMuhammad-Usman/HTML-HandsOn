henry cavil

Henry William Dalgliesh Cavill is a British actor. He was born on May 5, 1983, in Jersey, Channel Islands, UK. He is known for his roles in the DC Extended Universe as Superman in the 2013 film Man of Steel, the 2016 film Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice, and the 2017 film Justice League. He also played the role of Charles Brandon in the TV series The Tudors, and the role of Geralt of Rivia in the Netflix series The Witcher.

can yaman

Can Yaman is a Turkish actor, model, and lawyer. He was born on November 8, 1989, in Istanbul, Turkey. He is known for his roles in the Turkish TV series Erkenci Kus, Bay Yanlis, and Viola come il mare. He has won several awards for his performances, including the Best Actor award at the 2019 Murex d'Or Awards.

Burak Özçivit

Burak Özçivit is a Turkish actor and model. He was born on December 24, 1984, in Istanbul, Turkey. He is known for his roles in the Turkish TV series Çarpisma, Kara Sevda, and Kuruluş: Osman. He has won several awards for his performances, including the Best Actor award at the 2017 Turkey Youth Awards.
