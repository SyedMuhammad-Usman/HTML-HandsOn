Non-breaking Space Example

(&nbsp;)is used to insert a space that should not be collapsed or wrapped by the browser. It ensures that the space remains visible and the text stays together on the same line.

This sentence contains non-breaking spaces that prevent the browser from collapsing the spaces.

Without non-breaking spaces, multiple spaces     are collapsed into a single space.
